About

InsulatorPlus is an AI-based technology company that offers the real-time status monitoring about the remote insulators, the complete management of the insulator status, as well as predicting the status based on context-aware scenarios.

InsulatorPlus is founded by Shikai Gong, a senior student at Damien High School. He started the project from his research science project. He is passionate about coding and using technology to solve real-world problems.
